# Lost Packages and Non-Delivery Claims

**Document ID:** POL-LOST-001  
**Version:** 2026-01  
**Applies to:** Orders showing **delivered** but not received, or tracking stalled beyond carrier thresholds.

---

## SEC-LST-001 — Carrier Investigation Window

Customers must report **non-receipt** within **14 days** of the **stated delivery scan** (or last tracking update for stalled shipments). Reports after this window may require **escalation** and stronger evidence.

---

## SEC-LST-002 — “Delivered” but Not Received

Process:

1. Verify **address on order** matches customer claim.  
2. Check **carrier proof of delivery** (photo, signature, GPS).  
3. If POD shows **wrong address** or **neighbor delivery**, open carrier case; **replacement or refund** may follow carrier outcome.  
4. If POD shows **correct address** and customer denies receipt, **one** reshipment or refund may be approved after **internal fraud checks** (POL-FRD-001). Repeat claims trigger **account review**.

---

## SEC-LST-003 — Stalled / No Updates

If tracking has **no movement** for **7 business days** (domestic) or **14 business days** (international) beyond the estimated range:

- Open a **carrier trace**.  
- Offer **replacement** or **refund** if the carrier declares **lost** or cannot locate within **trace SLA**.

---

## SEC-LST-004 — Marketplace Shipments

**Seller-fulfilled** orders: PurpleCart facilitates the ticket but **seller policy** and carrier chosen by seller apply for timing. If the seller provides **no valid tracking**, treat as **non-shipment** per POL-MKT-001.

---

## SEC-LST-005 — High-Value Orders

Orders above **$500** (threshold subject to update) require **manager approval** for refund without return of goods when fraud score is elevated.
