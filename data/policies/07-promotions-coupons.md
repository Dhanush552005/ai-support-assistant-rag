# Promotions, Coupons, and Price Adjustments

**Document ID:** POL-PRM-001  
**Version:** 2026-01  
**Applies to:** Promotional codes, cart-level discounts, and loyalty rewards at checkout.

---

## SEC-PRM-001 — Coupon Eligibility

Coupons apply only to **eligible items** as stated in the promotion terms. Common exclusions:

- **Final sale** and **gift cards** (unless explicitly included)  
- **Marketplace** items if the promo is **first-party only** (see POL-MKT-001)  
- **Brands** or **categories** listed in fine print  

**One promo code per order** unless the promotion explicitly allows stacking.

---

## SEC-PRM-002 — Stacking Rules

When multiple discounts could apply:

1. **Automatic** site-wide discounts combine with **loyalty tier** discounts only where stated.  
2. **Coupon + automatic:** The system applies the **best single discount** for the customer unless terms allow combination.  
3. **Employee or partner codes** may not combine with public promotions.

---

## SEC-PRM-003 — Expiry and Retroactive Application

- Coupons are valid until **expiry date and time** in the customer’s **account timezone** at redemption, unless UTC is specified.  
- **Price protection / retroactive coupons** after purchase are **not** guaranteed; discretionary **partial credit** may be issued for **large** price drops within **7 days** on a case-by-case basis—**do not** promise as policy.

---

## SEC-PRM-004 — Refunds When Coupons Were Used

If an item purchased with a coupon is refunded:

- Refund amount is the **amount actually paid** for that line item.  
- **Coupon value** may be **forfeited** if the promotion terms state single-use and the order no longer meets minimums; system recalculates prorated refund.

---

## SEC-PRM-005 — Abusive Promotion Use

Bulk account creation, VPN abuse, or **reselling** purchased inventory using stacked promos may result in **order cancellation** and **account restriction** (POL-FRD-001).
