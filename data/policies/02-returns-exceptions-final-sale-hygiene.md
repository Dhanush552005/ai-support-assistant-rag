# Returns — Exceptions: Final Sale, Hygiene, and Intimate Items

**Document ID:** POL-RET-002  
**Version:** 2026-01  
**Applies to:** Product categories designated at checkout and in product detail pages.

---

## SEC-EXC-001 — Final Sale Items

Items marked **“Final Sale”** or **“Non-Returnable”** at the time of purchase **cannot** be returned for refund or exchange except where:

- The item is **defective** upon arrival (see POL-DIS-001), or  
- **Local consumer law** mandates a longer or mandatory return right (see POL-REG-001).

Final sale status is determined by **product listing at order placement**, not by later price changes or promotions.

---

## SEC-EXC-002 — Hygiene and Personal Care

For health and safety, the following are **non-returnable** once **opened** or **used**:

- Cosmetics, skincare, and fragrances  
- Oral care (toothbrushes, dental appliances where applicable)  
- Earplugs, nasal aspirators, and similar personal-insert products  
- Bedding and pillows **without** factory seals if opened  

**Sealed, unopened** hygiene products in original packaging may be returned within the standard window if the category is not otherwise excluded.

---

## SEC-EXC-003 — Intimate Apparel and Swimwear

Intimate apparel and swimwear may be returned only if:

- Hygiene strips and tags are **intact**, and  
- The item is **unworn** and in original packaging.

Items without intact hygiene protection are **not eligible** for return.

---

## SEC-EXC-004 — Customized and Personalized Products

Custom engraved, monogrammed, or made-to-order items are **final sale** unless the customization differs materially from what was ordered (manufacturing defect). Size or spelling errors **caused by customer-submitted text** are not grounds for return unless the preview was incorrect due to platform error.

---

## SEC-EXC-005 — Clearance and Open-Box

**Open-box** and **warehouse clearance** items are labeled at purchase. They may be final sale or subject to **restocking fees** as disclosed on the product page. Restocking fees, when applicable, are deducted from the refund before payment reversal.

---

## SEC-EXC-006 — Escalation

If a customer disputes final sale or hygiene classification, agents must verify:

1. Listing snapshot or order line attributes at purchase.  
2. Applicable regional rules (POL-REG-001).  

Do **not** override final sale for **subjective** reasons (e.g., “I changed my mind”) when policy and listing agree.
