# Shipping and Delivery

**Document ID:** POL-SHIP-001  
**Version:** 2026-01  
**Applies to:** Domestic and international shipments where PurpleCart or partners provide tracking.

---

## SEC-SHP-001 — Delivery Estimates

Displayed delivery dates are **estimates**, not guarantees, unless the order explicitly includes a **“Guaranteed delivery”** fee or program. Estimates account for **business days**, carrier capacity, and **shipping region** on the order.

Weather, customs, carrier disruptions, and incorrect addresses may delay delivery without automatic compensation unless **Guaranteed delivery** or **premium SLA** applies.

---

## SEC-SHP-002 — Shipping Regions

**Shipping region** on the order determines:

- Available carriers and methods  
- Import duties and taxes (disclosed at checkout where collectible)  
- Perishable handling rules (POL-RET-003)  

Customers are responsible for **address accuracy**. Redelivery fees may apply if the carrier charges for address correction.

---

## SEC-SHP-003 — Signature and Safe Drop

**Signature required** items cannot be left without signature unless the customer opts into carrier **authority to leave** where legally permitted. Lost-after-delivery claims require **evidence** (carrier photo, GPS) per POL-LOST-001.

---

## SEC-SHP-004 — Split Shipments

Items may ship separately. **Partial delivery** does not extend the return window for items already delivered unless the split causes **combined use** to be impossible (evaluated case by case).

---

## SEC-SHP-005 — Late Delivery — Standard Shipping

For **non-guaranteed** shipping:

- **No automatic refund** solely for lateness.  
- **Courtesy** partial credit may be offered for severe delays per internal guidelines; **do not** promise policy-level compensation unless escalation approves.

---

## SEC-SHP-006 — Guaranteed Delivery Programs

If **Guaranteed delivery** is purchased and missed:

- Refund of **shipping fee** or **service fee** attached to the guarantee, per program terms.  
- Product refund requires **return** or **non-receipt** investigation, not automatic solely for late arrival.
