# Disputes — Damaged or Incorrect Items

**Document ID:** POL-DIS-001  
**Version:** 2026-01  
**Applies to:** Items received **damaged**, **defective**, or **not as described** vs. what was ordered.

---

## SEC-DMG-001 — Reporting Window

Customers should report within **30 days** of delivery. Late reports may still be honored with **evidence** if the issue is **latent** (e.g., electronics failure after unboxing within reasonable use).

---

## SEC-DMG-002 — Evidence

Agents may require:

- **Photos** of packaging, shipping label, and damage  
- **Serial numbers** where applicable  
- **Lot numbers** for consumables  

**Do not** instruct customers to return **hazardous** damaged goods without a prepaid label and safety guidance.

---

## SEC-DMG-003 — Resolution Options

Eligible resolutions (based on inventory and region):

- **Full refund** including standard shipping paid on the order line (if applicable)  
- **Replacement** at no charge with prepaid return of defective unit when required  
- **Partial refund** if the customer **keeps** a salvageable item (e.g., cosmetic box damage only)

---

## SEC-DMG-004 — Incorrect Item Shipped

If the **wrong SKU** was shipped:

- **Prepaid return** label for the incorrect item  
- **Expedited ship** of correct item when in stock  
- **Full refund** if replacement is declined or unavailable  

Customer must **not** be charged restocking for **our** fulfillment error.

---

## SEC-DMG-005 — Marketplace vs First-Party

- **First-party (1P):** POL-DIS-001 applies fully.  
- **Marketplace:** Seller must respond within **48 hours**; if no response, **A-to-z style** protection may apply per POL-MKT-001 thresholds.

---

## SEC-DMG-006 — Conflict With Return Exceptions

**Final sale** and **hygiene** rules (POL-RET-002) **do not** block claims for **defective** or **not as described** items where consumer law or platform policy requires remedy. Document **defect** vs. **change of mind** clearly.
