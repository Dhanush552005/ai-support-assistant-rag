# Regional and International Rules

**Document ID:** POL-REG-001  
**Version:** 2026-01  
**Applies to:** Orders where **shipping region**, **billing country**, or **destination jurisdiction** affects legal rights.

---

## SEC-REG-001 — Hierarchy When Rules Conflict

When **platform policy**, **seller policy**, and **local law** conflict:

1. **Mandatory local consumer law** prevails for rights that cannot be waived.  
2. Otherwise **shipping region** rules in this document apply to **PurpleCart obligations**.  
3. **Seller-specific** terms apply to **marketplace** fulfillment per POL-MKT-001.

Agents must **escalate** ambiguous cross-border cases to **legal/compliance queue**.

---

## SEC-REG-002 — United States — State Variations

Certain states require **extended return windows** for specific categories or **restocking fee** disclosures. **Checkout** and **order confirmation** must reflect applicable disclosures. If a customer cites **state law**, verify **ship-to** address and escalate if not in training materials.

---

## SEC-REG-003 — European Union / UK (Illustrative)

For orders shipped to **EU/UK** where our terms apply:

- **Withdrawal rights** may apply for distance sales (exceptions for sealed goods opened, perishables, etc.).  
- **Warranty** claims may follow **local statutory** periods longer than our standard warranty.

**Do not** invent specific day counts; cite **published regional help pages** or escalate.

---

## SEC-REG-004 — Import Duties and Taxes

**DDP vs. DDU** is shown at checkout. Customers refusing delivery due to **unexpected duties** when **DDU** was selected may receive **refund minus** return shipping and duties per carrier outcome.

---

## SEC-REG-005 — Prohibited or Restricted Items

Some regions **restrict** categories (alcohol, supplements, electronics). If an order is **cancelled** for compliance, **full payment refund** applies; **do not** promise compensation beyond refund unless **published** exception.
