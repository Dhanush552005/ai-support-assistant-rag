# Disputes — Missing Items and Short Shipments

**Document ID:** POL-DIS-002  
**Version:** 2026-01  
**Applies to:** Orders where **fewer items** arrived than invoiced, or **empty** or **partial** packages.

---

## SEC-MIS-001 — Reporting

Report within **14 days** of delivery. Include **unboxing photo** of all received items with **visible packing slip** if provided.

---

## SEC-MIS-002 — Verification Steps

1. Compare **invoice line count** vs. **carrier weight** and **package dimensions** from manifest.  
2. Check **split shipment** status—missing item may be **in transit** on a second package.  
3. If single package and weight matches **full order**, investigate **pick/pack error** vs. **carrier tampering**.

---

## SEC-MIS-003 — Resolution

If investigation supports **short pick** or **loss in transit**:

- **Refund** for missing line item **or**  
- **Ship missing item** if in stock  

**Shipping fees** for the order may be **partially refunded** if the missing item was a **major** portion of the order value (internal threshold applies).

---

## SEC-MIS-004 — Customer Claims Repeated Short Packs

Multiple claims within **90 days** trigger **fraud review** (POL-FRD-001). Agents must **not** accuse the customer; use neutral language and follow investigation outcome.

---

## SEC-MIS-005 — Subscription / Multi-Box

For **meal kits** or **multi-box** subscriptions, missing component resolution follows **subscription terms**; **one-time** credit may be issued for minor missing ingredients without full box refund.
