# Returns and Refunds — General Policy

**Document ID:** POL-RET-001  
**Version:** 2026-01  
**Applies to:** All orders placed on PurpleCart and affiliated marketplaces unless a product-specific exception applies.

---

## SEC-RET-001 — Standard Return Window

Customers may initiate a return within **30 calendar days** of the **delivery date** shown in order tracking for eligible items. The return window opens on the date the carrier marks the shipment as delivered, not the date the customer opens the package.

For orders with split shipments, each item’s return window is calculated from **that item’s** delivery confirmation.

---

## SEC-RET-002 — Eligibility Requirements

To qualify for a refund or exchange under the standard policy, items must:

- Be in **original, unused condition** with tags attached where applicable.
- Include **all original packaging**, inserts, accessories, and bundled items.
- Not be listed under **non-returnable categories** (see documents POL-RET-002 through POL-RET-004).

Opened software, digital goods, and gift cards are evaluated under separate rules.

---

## SEC-RET-003 — Refund Methods

Refunds are issued to the **original payment method** when technically possible.

- **Credit/debit card:** 5–10 business days after the returned item is received and inspected.
- **Store credit:** Issued within 2 business days of inspection if the customer selects store credit or if the original payment method is closed.
- **Buy-now-pay-later (BNPL):** Adjustments follow the BNPL provider’s terms; we notify the provider when a return is approved.

Partial refunds may apply if items are returned incomplete or damaged due to customer misuse (see POL-DIS-001).

---

## SEC-RET-004 — Return Shipping Costs

- **Defective or incorrect item (our error):** We provide a prepaid label or refund reasonable return shipping up to the standard carrier rate for the customer’s region.
- **Customer remorse / change of mind:** Return shipping is the customer’s responsibility unless local law requires otherwise.
- **Promotional “free returns” campaigns:** Only orders explicitly tagged with the promotion at checkout qualify; standard rules apply after the campaign ends.

---

## SEC-RET-005 — Inspection and Denial

Returned items are inspected within **3 business days** of receipt. We may deny or partially approve a return if:

- The item shows wear, damage, or missing components not disclosed in the return request.
- The return falls under a **non-returnable** or **final sale** category.
- The return is outside the stated window without an approved exception.

Customers receive email notification of the outcome with reference to the applicable policy section.

---

## SEC-RET-006 — Exchanges

Exchanges are offered **subject to inventory**. If the desired size or color is unavailable, the customer may receive a refund or store credit per SEC-RET-003.
